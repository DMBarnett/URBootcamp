Origins


Formed in 1967 in London, England, Fleetwood Mac's lineup changed several times until their 1975 release of Fleetwood Mac. Reaching number 1 on the charts in the US, the band became one of the largest bands in the world during the 1970's.


The band was noted for its internal strife, as the couples in the band split up during the late 80's.
